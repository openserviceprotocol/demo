# CloudOps

> Enterprise managed cloud for demanding production environments.
> AWS and Azure, 24/7 monitoring and incident response, ISO 27001
> and SOC 2 certified. For companies where downtime is not an
> option. Based in Zurich, Switzerland.

## Available Services

- [Managed Hosting](/osp/services/managed-hosting.yaml): VMs and containers on AWS/Azure, fully managed, 99.9% SLA
- [24/7 Monitoring & Incident Response](/osp/services/monitoring.yaml): Proactive monitoring with guaranteed response time < 15 minutes
- [Security Audit](/osp/services/security-audit.yaml): Infrastructure security assessment and penetration testing

## Not Available

- Google Cloud Platform
- Software development
- On-premise hardware
- Projects under CHF 2,000/month

## Conditions

- Minimum contract: 12 months (managed hosting), none for audits
- Regions: DACH
- Languages: de, en
- Incident response: < 15 minutes (24/7)
