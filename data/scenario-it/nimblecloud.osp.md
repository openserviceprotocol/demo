# NimbleCloud

> Lean cloud hosting for SMEs and startups. AWS exclusively,
> deeply specialized and cost-optimized. Business-hours support
> that's sufficient when your application isn't a trading floor.
> Transparent, fairly priced, no overhead. Based in Berlin.

## Available Services

- [Managed AWS Hosting](/osp/services/aws-hosting.yaml): VMs and containers on AWS, managed during business hours, automated monitoring 24/7
- [AWS Migration](/osp/services/aws-migration.yaml): Migration from on-premise or other cloud providers to AWS
- [Cost Optimization](/osp/services/cost-optimization.yaml): Analysis and optimization of existing AWS infrastructure costs

## Not Available

- Azure, Google Cloud, or other providers (AWS only)
- 24/7 live support (automated monitoring runs 24/7, human response Mon-Fri 08-18 only)
- Windows Server (Linux only)
- Managed databases (we recommend AWS RDS directly)
- On-premise or hybrid cloud

## Conditions

- Minimum contract: 6 months
- No minimum volume — even single instances
- Regions: DACH
- Languages: de, en
- Support response time: < 1h (business hours)
