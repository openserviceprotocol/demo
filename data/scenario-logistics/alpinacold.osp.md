# AlpinaCold Logistics

> Specialist for temperature-controlled transport within
> Switzerland. Own refrigerated fleet for pharma, diagnostics,
> food, and blood products. Daily routes between all major Swiss
> cities. GDP and Swissmedic compliant. Based in Bern, Switzerland.

## Available Services

- [Pharma Express](/osp/services/pharma-express.yaml): Temperature-controlled 2-8°C, same-day nationwide, Swissmedic-compliant
- [Cool Chain LTL](/osp/services/cool-ltl.yaml): 1-10 pallets, temperature-controlled 2-25°C, next-day nationwide
- [Frozen Transport](/osp/services/frozen.yaml): -25°C to -15°C, unbroken cold chain documentation

## Not Available

- International transport (Switzerland only)
- Non-temperature-controlled transport
- Shipments over 10 pallets
- Warehousing and fulfillment
- Dangerous goods (except GDP-compliant pharma and diagnostics)
