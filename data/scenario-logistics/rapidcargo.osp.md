# RapidCargo

> Fast and cost-effective partial-load and groupage transport
> across Central Europe. Focus on speed and price. Ideal partner
> for time-critical, non-temperature-sensitive shipments.
> Own fleet of 180 vehicles. Headquartered in Frankfurt, Germany.

## Available Services

- [Express LTL](/osp/services/express-ltl.yaml): 3-12 pallets, 24-48h delivery, Central Europe
- [Standard LTL](/osp/services/standard-ltl.yaml): 3-18 pallets, 48-72h delivery, all of Europe
- [Direct Haul](/osp/services/direct.yaml): From 10 pallets, same-day possible if booked by 08:00

## Not Available

- Temperature-controlled transport
- Pharma logistics and GDP services
- Dangerous goods of any kind
- Overseas (road transport within Europe only)
- Warehousing

## Conditions

- Minimum order: CHF 350
- Regions: Switzerland, Germany, Austria, France, Benelux, Italy, Poland, Czech Republic
- Languages: de, en
- Quote response time: < 30 minutes (automated)

## Integration

- OSP Version: 0.1
- Contracting: available
- Delivery Tracking: available
- Settlement: invoice, token
