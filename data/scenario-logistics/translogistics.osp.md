# TransLogistics

> International transport and logistics specializing in temperature-
> controlled shipments for pharma, biotech, and food industries.
> GDP-certified. Europe-wide coverage, from 5 pallets to full
> truckloads. Headquartered in Basel, Switzerland.

## Available Services

- [LTL Transport](/osp/services/ltl-transport.yaml): Partial loads 5-15 pallets, temperature-controlled available, 48-96h across Europe
- [FTL Transport](/osp/services/ftl-transport.yaml): Dedicated truck, all temperature zones, 24-48h
- [Customs Clearance](/osp/services/customs.yaml): CH-EU import/export, certificates of origin

## Not Available

- Air freight and ocean freight
- Dangerous goods classes 1, 6, 7
- Single pallets under 5 units
- Destinations outside Europe

## Conditions

- Minimum order: CHF 800
- Regions: Switzerland, EU, UK, Norway
- Languages: de, en
- Quote response time: < 2h during business hours (CET)
