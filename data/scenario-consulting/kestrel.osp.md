# Kestrel Advisory

> Management consulting for strategic and operational challenges
> in the mid-market. From market expansion to post-merger
> integration to restructuring. Broad capability set, pragmatic,
> implementation-oriented. Offices in Zurich, Munich, and Vienna.

## Available Services

- [Growth Strategy](/osp/services/growth-strategy.yaml): Growth strategies including market expansion, diversification, portfolio optimization
- [Post-Merger Integration](/osp/services/pmi.yaml): Integration after acquisitions — culture, processes, synergies
- [Operational Restructuring](/osp/services/restructuring.yaml): Cost optimization and process redesign in crisis situations

## Not Available

- IT implementation
- Recruitment and executive search
- Legal and tax advisory
- Pure research projects without strategy component
- Companies under CHF 15M revenue
