# EuroGrowth Partners

> Data-driven strategy consulting for European market expansion.
> We help DACH companies enter new markets across Europe — from
> Scandinavia to Iberia, from the UK to Eastern Europe. Combining
> quantitative market modeling with a local partner network in
> 18 European countries. Based in Munich.

## Available Services

- [EU Market Entry](/osp/services/eu-market-entry.yaml): Market analysis, distribution model design, partner identification for EU markets
- [Competitive Intelligence Europe](/osp/services/competitive-intel.yaml): Systematic competitor analysis across European target markets
- [Distribution Setup](/osp/services/distribution.yaml): Building and optimizing distribution structures in EU countries

## Not Available

- Markets outside Europe (no US, no Asia)
- M&A advisory and due diligence
- Operational restructuring
- Companies under CHF 10M revenue
